Who We Are

Vehycles is reshaping Africa’s auto-aftermarket by building a market-network, a digital ecosystem designed to transform how vehicle owners manage their vehicle lifecycle needs. Our platform empowers users with full control and seamless access to essential services, including documentation, specialized maintenance, replacements, and auto-addons. By integrating these critical components into one unified solution, Vehycles simplifies and enhances the vehicle ownership experience, setting a new standard for efficiency, transparency, and convenience across the continent.

The App

The platform categorizes services into four main areas: documentation (Registration, licenses, insurance, compliance tracking), maintenance (specialized repairs, routine checks, service reminders), replacements (parts and components replacements), and auto-addons (upgrades, enhanced experience). By integrating these resources into one user-friendly platform, Vehycles empowers vehicle owners and fleet managers
with the tools and information needed to manage their vehicles efficiently.

To provide vehicle owners in Africa with a comprehensive digital platform that offers full control and seamless access to essential services throughout the lifetime of their vehicles.

To revolutionize Africa’s auto-aftermarket by building a market-network that simplifies and enhances vehicle ownership, offering end-to-end, sterling experiences through a robust and integrated digital ecosystem.
